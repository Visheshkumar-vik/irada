About

Most policy never reaches
the women it's about.

Research on gender equality in India keeps getting better. The women it's written for almost never see it. It stays inside policy reports and conferences, then quietly disappears.

Insight that doesn't reach people is just paper.

Iradda was started by an engineer and a policy researcher to fix that. Ishika interned at NITI Aayog (the Prime Minister's Office of India). Vishesh studies engineering at RMIT in Melbourne. Together we're building the missing layer: the place where research actually becomes useful.

Our Mission

Closing the distance
between what's known
and what's done.

There has never been more research on what would help Indian women. Most of it never reaches them. We're building the framework that does.

What we believe
